Alex St. John Smythe

White Bishop of Checkmate

Game: DC Universe Online
Name:  Alexander St. John Smythe ne Alexander Godberd
Birthplace:  Nottinghamshire, England
Origin:
Alex was born to a fine family tradition, going all the way back to a man society remembers as "Robin Hood".
The Godberd family busines is theaft, but not low class robbery.  It is a point of pride that no one is ever hurt in a Godberd caper.  Indeed Alex refuses to carry or use a gun, though in family tradition he is an expert bowman.  He's also an expert martial artist.

Alex was into his late twenties when he got reckless and got caught.   Checkmate offered him a deal.  Work for us and we will get you out, pay you a salery, and use your abilities for the good of the world.  As part of the deal, they gave him a new alias as "Alexander St. John Smyth", a UN expense account, and rewarding work.  His expertise and analyzing and planning missions as well as his abiltiy to blend into almost any social milleau eventually brought him to the position of White Queen's Bishop.,  The bishops are the advisers to the royalty, who are the senior officers in charge of Checkmate.  Only royalty out-rank a bishop.
